# How-to: Process a Payment

Use this guide when a participant hands over cash and you need to mark their registration as paid.

**Role required:** Clerk or Admin

---

## Mark a single registration as paid

1. In the Clerk or Admin view, find the registration in the list
2. Click the registration row to open the detail panel
3. Toggle the **Pago** switch to ON
4. The status badge changes from **Pendente** to **Pago** immediately

The change saves to the database automatically. No separate save button is needed.

---

## Mark payment for a family group

Each family member has their own registration and must be marked paid individually:

1. Find the primary registrant's registration
2. Mark it as **Pago**
3. Search for each family member by name or registration number
4. Mark each one as **Pago**

> To find all members of a family group quickly, search for the primary registrant's name — family members registered in the same batch will appear in the list sorted by registration number in sequence.

---

## Mark a registration as exempt (Isento)

If a participant should not pay (event decision, not automatic Pastor/Ungido exemption):

1. Open the registration detail panel
2. Toggle **Isento** to ON — the fee is set to $0 and the status becomes **Isento**

Note: Pastors and Ungidos are automatically marked as Isento when registered. You don't need to do this manually for them.

---

## Undo a payment (mark as unpaid)

1. Open the registration detail panel
2. Toggle **Pago** back to OFF
3. The status returns to **Pendente**

---

## What if the participant paid but I can't find their registration?

Ask for their registration number (format: `PREFIX-YYYYMMDD-NNNN`). Enter it in the search bar in the Clerk view. If they don't have the number, they can look it up via **Consultar Inscrição** on the home screen.
